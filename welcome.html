Tonight's Record
Fleetwood Mac — Rumours (1977)
Pop Rock  ·  Warner Bros.  ·  Producers: Ken Caillat, Richard Dashut & Fleetwood Mac
Recorded while the band was falling apart in real time — two couples breaking up simultaneously, everyone furious at everyone else. The result was 11 tracks of pop perfection. Over 40 million copies sold. Chaos is productive.
Their eleventh studio album proved to be the band's enduring masterpiece — it snagged a Grammy and became the fastest-selling LP of all time. Produced with smooth electric and acoustic guitar, it comes easy on the ears even as the lyrics quietly devastate. This is peak Fleetwood Mac: the oh-so-pleasant soundtrack to your life falling apart.
When to spin: Tarot-reading night. Beaded curtains optional but encouraged.
